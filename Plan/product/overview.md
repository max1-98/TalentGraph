# Product — Overview

## Product Thesis

TalentGraph is a talent matching platform built around one insight: the
existing job search model is broken for both sides. Candidates blast
applications into a void; employers wade through noise. The root problem is
**candidate-initiated contact at the wrong signal-to-noise ratio**.

TalentGraph inverts this. Employers post a role; the algorithm selects the
strongest-matched candidates and notifies them: "You're a strong fit for this
role — interested?" The candidate decides whether to engage. The employer never
cold-contacts anyone.

This is not a job board. It is not a recruiter DM platform. It is a
**matchmaker**: it acts on both sides simultaneously, surfacing mutual fit
before either party expends effort.

---

## Two User Types

**Talent** — professionals seeking their next role, career clarity, or
skill development. Primary benefits: match notifications, gap analysis, CV
creation, career path intelligence.

**Clients** — employers, hiring teams, or solo founders building a team.
Primary benefit: a curated, interested candidate pool delivered by the
algorithm after posting a role. No sourcing required.

A user can hold both roles at once (e.g., a founder hiring while also open to
opportunities). Role context is inferred from action, not locked to an account
type.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Reverse Outreach
The algorithm — not the employer — initiates candidate contact. The platform
notifies candidates selected by the match engine, preserving candidate
agency and employer signal quality.

### Match Transparency
Every match notification includes a score breakdown: why the algorithm
selected this candidate for this role. Neither side operates on mystery scores.

### Individual Value Tools
Before any employer ever joins the platform, a candidate receives independent
value: gap analysis against a target role, CV creation and improvement,
career path projections, and role recommendations. The network enhances these;
it is not a prerequisite.

## Competitive Positioning

| Dimension | Job Boards | LinkedIn | TalentGraph |
|---|---|---|---|
| Initiation | Candidate applies cold | Recruiter DMs cold | Algorithm selects; candidate decides |
| Match quality | Keyword filter | Social graph | Multi-signal tensor scoring |
| Solo value | None | Some (profile) | High (gap analysis, CV, career path) |
| Candidate agency | Low (apply and wait) | Low (be found or search) | High (accept or ignore curated matches) |

---

## Positioning Statement

TalentGraph delivers high-signal career matches to professionals without
requiring them to search, and delivers pre-interested, algorithm-selected
candidates to employers without requiring them to source.

---

## Success Metrics (framing, not targets)

- **Activation:** candidate completes first gap analysis or receives first
  match preview within the onboarding session
- **Engagement:** weekly match digest open rate; feed session frequency
- **Network health:** ratio of posted roles to qualified candidate pool per role
- **Outcome quality:** hire and shortlist rates per match notification sent
